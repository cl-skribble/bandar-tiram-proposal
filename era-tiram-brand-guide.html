Brand Identity & Direction

The Synthesis
of Eras.

Era Tiram is a landmark township development by JLand (Johor Land Group), conceived as a new urban standard that merges the region's cultural heritage with forward-looking, human-centred design.

The brand draws its identity from two converging forces: the deep-rooted heritage of the Tiram district, and a bold vision for modern living that places human experience at its foundation. The result is a township that does not merely build — it cultivates.

Era Tiram is positioned not as another property development, but as a living ecosystem — a synthesis of community, nature, culture, and infrastructure, designed around the anatomy of how people truly live.

Pillar 01
Modern Heritage
Not erasing the past, but elevating it to build the future. Contemporary living is merged with historic roots to create an ultimate modern lifestyle right within the city limits.
Pillar 02
Human-Centred
Every design decision is inspired by the human form and how communities move, gather, and thrive. The city's foundational pillars mirror the anatomy of daily living.
Pillar 03
Clean Modernism
A culturally iconic environment built on sustainable, minimal principles. The new urban standard for Johor — functional, refined, and responsible.

Logo System

The Logomark

The Era Tiram mark is a geometric seal constructed from four interlocking ideas: the branching pattern of human bloodflow, the letter E for "Era," the letter T for "Tiram," and a leaf motif symbolising sustainability and growth.


The resulting form reads simultaneously as a tree canopy, a cultural weave, and a bold typographic ligature — anchoring the brand in both nature and place.

Brand Voice Attributes

    Purposeful language. Every word earns its place. No hyperbole, no filler. The brand speaks only when it has something real to say.
  • →
    Rooted
    Anchored in place, culture, and community. References to Tiram's heritage feel organic, not performative.

    Optimistic about the future without being blind to the present. The brand invites — it does not sell.
  • →

    Warm, accessible, and grounded. Speaks to real people and their real lives, not abstract lifestyle aspirations.

Brand Applications

⬛
Exterior Hoarding
Logo centred on forest green background. Tagline set in light-weight Cormorant below.
Minimum clear space = 1× logomark height on all sides. White logo on dark ground only.
◫
Wayfinding / Signage
Dark brown (#3B2A1E) pylon with cream logo. Directional text in Jost Light, tracked.
Precinct name appears below master logo in matching tracked uppercase style.
◻
Stationery
Business cards: ivory stock, forest green logo. Reverse: green ground, cream type.
Font: Jost Light for contact information. Generous white space throughout.
⬚
Uniforms & PPE
Forest green jacket/shirt with cream logomark embroidered on chest. White hardhat with full logo lockup.
Embroidery: cream thread on green. Hardhat: full colour print or deboss.
◱
Merchandise
Tote bags, notebooks, umbrellas: forest green base with cream logomark. Minimal decoration — logo is the hero.
Tote: 100% cotton, natural or forest green. Screen print preferred.
▣
Digital Screens
Sales gallery displays and digital OOH: full-bleed photography with logo overlaid in cream. Tagline in Cormorant Italic.
Minimum digital logo size: 120px height. Ensure contrast ratio ≥ 4.5:1.
09

Logo Usage Rules

Clear Space
  • ✓Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the "E" in "ERA" on all four sides of the logo.
  • ✓On outdoor formats, double the clear space minimum.
  • ✓The logomark alone (without wordmark) may be used at minimum 32px / 8mm.
Approved Backgrounds
  • ✓Ivory (#F8F5ED) — preferred background for print collateral.
  • ✓Forest Green (#2B4535) — primary brand background.
  • ✓Dark Brown (#3B2A1E) — wayfinding and environmental signage.
  • ✓White (#FFFFFF) — digital use only.
  • ✓Full-bleed photography — use cream reversed logo at sufficient contrast.
Never Do
  • ✗Do not stretch or distort the logo in any dimension.
  • ✗Do not rotate the logo.
  • ✗Do not apply drop shadows, gradients, or outlines to the logomark.
  • ✗Do not place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without sufficient contrast overlay.
  • ✗Do not change the logo colours to anything outside the approved palette.
  • ✗Do not separate the wordmark letters from their approved spacing.
  • ✗Do not use the precinct sub-brand logos independently as master brand.
Minimum Sizes
  • ✓Print — full lockup: 25mm width minimum.
  • ✓Digital — full lockup: 120px width minimum.
  • ✓Logomark only (no wordmark): 16mm / 80px minimum.
  • ✓Embroidery: 20mm width minimum.
