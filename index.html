Act 03 — Brand Platform · Pillar 02
Regenerative.

Era Tiram doesn't consume its environment — it regenerates it. Every resource that enters the township cycles back through it. Nothing is wasted, including the water, the food, or the community.

5-Diamond LCCF 2030
45% carbon reduction
from baseline by 2030.

Waste to Wealth: only 5% of township waste reaches landfill. The rest becomes compost, energy, or raw materials — circling back into the community.

The Four Cycles
Water
Rain → roof harvesting → bioswale → detention pond → natural irrigation → back to rain. The Education & Innovation Walk follows every step.
Food
Urban Farm rooftop → Bazaar → food waste → community composting → Kebun Orchards → harvest → Bazaar. Farm-to-table at township scale.
Carbon
Solar PV on every home + natural ventilation design → reduced grid load → 56,280 native trees → 0.2°C microclimate cooling → LCCF certification.
Community
Young Entrepreneur → First Home Owner → Lifetime Home Owner — the same person, the same township, ageing in place without ageing out.

The Synthesis
of Eras.

Era Tiram is a landmark township development by JLand, conceived as a new urban standard that merges the region's cultural heritage with forward-looking, human-centred design.

A living ecosystem — a synthesis of community, nature, culture, and infrastructure, designed around the anatomy of how people truly live.

Pillar 01
Modern Heritage
Not erasing the past, but elevating it — contemporary living merged with the historic roots of Tiram to create an ultimate modern lifestyle within the city limits.
Pillar 02
Human-Centred
Every design decision inspired by the human form and how communities move, gather, and thrive. The city's foundational pillars mirror the anatomy of daily living.
Pillar 03
Clean Modernism
A culturally iconic environment built on sustainable, minimal principles — the new urban standard for Johor: functional, refined, and responsible.
