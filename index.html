The Brand Landscape

How Johor's top townships position themselves — and where the white space lives.

Eco Botanic
"Live in a Botanical Garden"
Nature-first township in Iskandar Malaysia. Botanical garden as centrepiece, lush greenery as identity. Strong lifestyle pull for families.
Horizon Hills
Resort Golf Living
Premium gated township built around an 18-hole golf course. Resort-style amenities, lake views, strong appeal to upgraders and Singapore-facing buyers.
Puteri Harbour
International Waterfront Living
Marina-front township by UEM Sunrise. Positioned for affluent buyers and expatriates. International lifestyle, waterfront address, premium price point.
Bandar Tiram Today
No defined positioning
Competing on location specs and price. No brand story. No lifestyle promise. No headline.
The gap no one owns: Authentic Johor identity — rooted in community, ready for the future. That's the territory Bandar Tiram can claim.
